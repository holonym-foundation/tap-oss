# Examples

Add your TAP credentials to your agent once — in its context, memory, or by telling it to remember them (e.g. in CLAUDE.md or a skill):

```text
TAP API key: <API key from the dashboard>
TAP instructions: https://proxy.tap.human.tech/instructions
```

Then use any of these prompts as-is.

---

## Triage my inbox

```text
Triage my inbox — archive newsletters, flag anything that needs a reply, and draft responses to the urgent ones.
```

---

## Pay invoices from email

```text
Check my email for any unpaid invoices and pay them on Wise.
```

---

## Domain migration

```text
Migrate all our company domains to Cloudflare DNS, update the SSL certificates on AWS, and trigger Vercel redeployments for any affected services.
```

---

## Feature requests to Notion

```text
Check my Slack messages for any new feature requests and create a ticket for each one on my engineering board in Notion.
```

---

## Bank sweep

```text
Check my Mercury balance. If it's over $50k, move the excess into the highest-yield account.
```

---

## Failed payments

```text
Find all failed Stripe payments from the last 7 days, look up each customer's email, and draft a recovery email for each one.
```

---

## Nudge stale PRs

```text
Find any GitHub pull requests that have been waiting for review for more than 2 days and post a polite nudge on each one.
```

---

## Telegram digest

```text
Read my Telegram messages from the last 24 hours and give me a digest — what needs a response, what's just noise, and anything time-sensitive.
```

---

## Deployment audit

```text
Check my Vercel deployments from the last 24 hours. Summarize any failures and tell me which services are affected.
```

---

## Multi-service morning briefing

```text
Give me a morning briefing: Mercury balance and any large transactions, unread emails that need action, and whether any Vercel deployments failed overnight.
```
